    Privacy Policy

    Learn how ScamSafe protects your privacy and information.

    1. Introduction

    ScamSafe values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our cybersecurity awareness and education platform.

    2. Information We Collect

    • Anonymous learning progress and activity data (no login required).
    • User-submitted scam cases or feedback text.
    • Browser and device data for improving site performance.

    3. How We Use the Information

    We use collected information to:

    • Provide personalized learning feedback and AI scam analysis.
    • Improve scam detection accuracy through aggregated, anonymized data.
    • Maintain platform security and monitor system usage.

    4. Data Storage and Security

    All data is securely stored on Monash University–approved servers. No personal identifiers are collected, and regular security reviews are conducted to ensure data integrity.

    5. Third-Party Data Sources

    The platform integrates publicly available scam data from Scamwatch and the Australian Cyber Security Centre (ACSC) for educational and visualization purposes only.

    6. User Rights

    Users can request the deletion of their submitted cases or feedback. ScamSafe allows full use of its learning functions anonymously without login.

    Privacy Policy Terms of Service

    This website is maintained by ScamSafe as a public welfare initiative. Information provided is for educational purposes only. We are dedicated to preventing cybersecurity fraud through community awareness and education.
